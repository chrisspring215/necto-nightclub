Frequency Saturday

About the Night

Join us every Saturday at the hottest club night in Michigan as DJ Hardy spins Top 40, Dance & House in the Main Room with host MC Yoda.

A variety of different themes are offered in the Red Room each week. Check our calendar for details for all Saturday night Red Room events.

Voted Best Dance Club by Detroit A-List

Voted Best Dance Club & Best Place to Celebrate Your 21st Birthday by Current Magazine

Voted Best Night Club by AnnArbor.com

Dress Code:

No athletic wear (some game day exceptions)

No excessively baggy clothing (no tuck-ins, shirts cannot pass your front pockets)

No headgear (exceptions for ball caps and stylish hats)
