April 6th, 2015

Goth Prom 2015

About The Event:

Once again it is that special time of the year where we all dress our best, get together with our closest friends and have a wonderful night filled with the music we all love. Hosted by DJ Jinx and MC Yoda. DJ Voyager kicks the night before Void 6 and Jinx take over upstairs. DJ's Sofa King and Madisi command the Red Room all night!

FREE ADMISSION ALL NIGHT if you dress up. We will of course have our Prom picture station set up so you have something to remember this special night.

Event Info:

  • Doors

    9pm

  • Cover

    Free in formal attire / $1 before 10, $3 after

  • Other

    18+ w/valid ID
