Attak and Friends

Join DJ ATTAK every 1st Thursday for ATTAK & Friends in the Red Room at Necto Nightclub.

This month Attak will be joined by his friends Thunder St. Clair & The Mailman.

About The Artist:

Known for his high-energy Dubstep/Bass music sets, Attak is rising to the top of the midwest edm DJ scene. His sets can range from deep, glitched out bass music to booty bouncing trap and everything in between. Attak has opened for a massive list of headliners including PANTyRAiD, Excision, Steve Aoki, Skrillex, Flux Pavilion, Benny Benassi, Rusko, Mimosa, Zeds Dead, RL Grime, 12th Planet and many more. He also has an aggressive bass side project, DUB TERRORISTS, with longtime friend N:Coder.

Attak brings a fresh energetic performance that will leave dancefloors in shambles, always ready to make your ass shake. Be on the lookout for upcoming DJ sets, mixes and production work.
