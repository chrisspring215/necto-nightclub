Factory Monday

About the Night

Established in 2004, Factory is an internationally known Goth-Industrial night hosted by DJ Jinx & MC Yoda. Jinx is joined in the booth by rotating guest DJs Darkschoir, Void6, Skellie Lafleur and Data Rom spinning Industrial, Synthpop, Aggrotech, Alternative & EBM in the Main Room. In the Red Room, DJ SofaKing & Madisi spin banging EDM & Electro house.

Best known for it's unique theme parties, among the annual favorites are Halfway to Halloween, Goth Prom, Pure Pwnage and Heroes vs Villains. Factory has brought in special guest DJs such as Andy Laplegua, Ronan Harris, Twiggy Ramirez, Mark Jackson, Joe Letz, Nero Bellum and END: the DJ.

We have hosted numerous live concerts as well under the Factory banner. Artists that have performed live include: VNV Nation, Combichrist, Aesthetic Perfection, Faderhead, X-Rx, BlakOpz, Suicide Commando, Icon Of Coil, Panic Lift, Surgyn, Straftanz, [:SITD:] & Mindphazer.
