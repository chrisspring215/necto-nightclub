About the Club

Overview

The only place in Michigan that parties like Vegas is nestled in historic, downtown Ann Arbor. Like its city, Necto Nightclub is stylish, unique, and packed to the brim with character - featuring a different theme every night. Accordingly, the club readily caters to diverse patrons including college students, electronic music lovers, the LGBTQ+ community, industrial/goth enthusiasts, popular music fans, and VIP guests.

The area’s largest and longest running nightclub includes state-of-the-art audio, with a lighting and video system cleverly complementing our veteran DJs. Its multilevel complex regularly hosting national & international artists.

Necto is dedicated to creating a safe, lively, and unforgettable environment that is unrivaled in the state. Our professional security, exceptional bar staff, and competitive prices help us to achieve our goal of providing the best nightlife experience to all our guests.

We are constantly evolving and never stop improving to exceed your expectations. Please feel free to contact us and add us on social media.

LAYOUT

Necto Nightclub spans three floors, consisting of 5 bars, 3 sections, 2 dancefloors, multiple VIP areas, and a (patron-favorite) open air patio.

The Main Room boasts 3 bars, a mezzanine, VIP area, and the largest dance floor in the region. The famous Red Room is located downstairs, and most frequently provides an auxiliary dance area, but can also host smaller engagements or serve as a lounge area. The heated patio is open all year and presents a more intimate setting in which guests can easily converse and relax.

All parts of Necto are available for private event rentals and are subject to limited availability.

POLICIES

  • AGE REQUIRMENT

    Necto Night club is an 18 and up venue.

  • ID REQUIRMENT

    Valid ID is required to enter the club. Drivers license, passport, military ID, and state issued IDs work best.

  • Credit Cards and Cash

    We accept Visa, Master Card, AMEX and cash at the bar. Cash only at the door. ATM located on premises.
