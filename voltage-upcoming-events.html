Voltage Events

Since its inception, Necto Voltage strives to deliver sensational electronic music events. Featuring a solid array of international artists, from up and comers to heavy hitters, with genres ranging from the ethereal to bass music. Each event is uniquely tailored to provide an optimal experience for our guests. Past events have included headliners such as Jeff Mills, Avicii, Zedd, Hardwell, Porter Robinson, Steve Aoki, Above & Beyond, Excision, Dada Life, and many more!

Check listings for our upcoming events on our ticket site located at www.necto.com/tix. Ticket prices may vary.
