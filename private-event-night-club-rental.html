Event Rentals

The Necto Nightclub is available for private event rental. Necto can provide; state of the art sound, video and lighting, a full complement of bar staff, private security and other amities.

Available dates are limited and are restricted to 18 and up events only.

The club is located 40 minutes west of Detroit in Downtown Ann Arbor, Michigan, one block from the campus of the University of Michigan, and close to the campuses of Eastern Michigan University, Concordia University, and Washtenaw Community College.

For information regarding bottle service, booth rental or other services available during normal events, please visit our reservations & bottle service page.

Please contact us directly for further information and availability.
